# OpenReed

## About Us
OpenReed is an open-source initiative focused on 3D-printed tools, machines, and accessories for double reed instruments, emphasizing accessibility and affordability.

### Project Goals

- **Accessibility**: Make double reed tools easily available to learners and musicians
- **Affordability**: Minimize costs by combining 3D-printed parts with standard hardware
- **Open Source**: Freely share designs and knowledge for the benefit of the double reed community


### Design Philosophy

- **Open-Source**: Every design is created using open-source tools and shared openly with the community.
- **Script-Based**: Designs are defined by code rather than manual CAD operations, enabling reproducibility and effective version control.
- **Parametric**: Models are built with adjustable parameters, so one script can generate variants for different instruments, sizes, or printers.

OpenReed primarily relies on [OpenSCAD](https://openscad.org/) and [Python](https://www.python.org/) for design and development.



## Getting Our Products

You can obtain OpenReed products in one of two ways:
- **Build It Yourself**: Print the parts and assemble the product yourself. Visit the product pages through the navigation bar and follow the build guide.

- **Buy a Prebuilt Unit**: Purchase a ready-to-use product from [our stores](#stores). Every purchase helps support the project 🤗.
